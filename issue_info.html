Soul Whispers

Author's Name: Rinni Mahajan

About the Author: About the Author: Writing is my passion and my writing journey started long before when I was in high school. Sacred Heart High School Sidhpur, Dharamshala laid that foundation, and it made me realised my passion. I always dreamt of writing a book, but I didn't get courage to pick my pen and start writing because these days budding authors frame the works of renowned authors as theirs. This tradition boils me from within, but my heart tries to pacify me. Writing is an art, which only evolves with time. Writing does not happen overnight and that is why when I started writing "Soul Whispers" I knew, I am taking the leap of faith and I can either fall or swim. Leaving the decision with readers to decide whether this leap of faith survived the fall. In case, it does, then my dream stands accomplished. I am Lead Technical Writer at NXP Semiconductors with almost 9 years of experience in Technical Writing. Before NXP, I worked with companies like Interra Systems, Thales, Cognizant, and Fiserv. I am holder of following awards in my professional career: Triple Crown award by Toastmasters International, Star Performer of the Quarter, Cheers for Peers by Inter and Intra Team, Best Speaker at Society of Technical Communication/Technical Writers, and Rising Star of the Year. Besides, I am the chapter head of Delhi NCR region for Information Developers Foundation.

Description of the Book:

Soul Whispers is about the journey of soul from annihilation to rapture. There comes a phase in life when one is suffering due to the veil of illusion (Maya). The suffering in this phase occurs because one is lost in worldly riches and mind is unstable. When this veil gets uplifted, one realises the truth. This truth is an ultimate truth that connects all souls with source and brings immense unconditional love. The term Soul Whispers is coined to depict the evolution of soul on its journey from physical realm to higher conscience. It brings in light all the emotions or feelings that soul is prone to in physical and astral realm.
