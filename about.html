About Us

Who we are

'Soul Connection', the quarterly, multilingual e-magazine promoting Northeast India’s indigenous poetry, arts, and literature is published by the Guwahati Grand Poetry Festival, Northeast India's first and only of its kind poetry, arts, and literature forum based at Guwahati, Assam, India. The e-magazine was launched on 19 September 2020, at Guwahati.

We strongly believe that poetry is a powerful medium that can help us break all barriers, and 'Soul Connection' intends to do exactly that, making it a collectible of sorts with poems from different generations of poets to inspire and emulate.

Mandate:

Extending a much-needed platform to poets, artistes, and writers and promoting young talents of Northeast India by providing them maximum scope to express their creativity without bias.
